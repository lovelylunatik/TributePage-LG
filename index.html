Lorelei Gilmore

Background

Born on April 25, 1968 to Richard Gilmore and Emily Gilmore in Hartford, Connecticut. At age 16, Lorelei discovered she was pregnant with her boyfriend, Christopher Hayden, baby. While Emily and Richard wished for Lorelei and Christopher to be married and raise the baby together, Christopher's parents wanted Lorelei to have an abortion. Lorelei, who grew up feeling like an outcast in a world filled with money and greed, she wanted a different life.

On October 4, 1985, Lorelei went into labor and gave birth to her daughter, whom in a demoral high gave her the name Lorelei (Rory) Gilmore. On that day Lorelei decided to start a new life for her and her daughter and left her parents home. She found herself in the small, pictureque town of Stars Hallow and was given a job and a place to live by Mia, the owner of the Independence Inn.

Lorelei worked hard as a single mother, eventually getting her and Rory a home. She also worked hard at the Inn and eventually became promoted all the way to the Manager. There she also met her best friend, Sookie St. James, a extremely talented but somewhat clumsy chef. The town of Stars Hollow also helped Lorelei in raising Rory with characters like Ms. Patty, Luke Danes and Babette doing everything they can to help support their small family.
